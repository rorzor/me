About Me

I have been immersed in drone systems and data since 2014, capturing high‑resolution imagery and engineering robust workflows for industrial inspection. Over the years, my curiosity for what happens after the data lands pushed me further into software engineering, where I discovered a passion for Python‑powered machine learning. Today I architect scalable ML solutions that turn pixels and point clouds into actionable insights—while still indulging my love of flight.

When I'm not wrangling code or datasets, you'll catch me experimenting with game design, learning about new technologies, and trying to better understand what makes us who we are.
